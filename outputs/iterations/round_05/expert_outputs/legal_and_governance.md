# Expert analysis: legal_and_governance

## Findings (evidence)
- The 6- and 8-year gimnázium forms are structurally anchored in the national public education act (Nkt.) and formal maintainer agreements; their phase-out requires legislative amendment, multi-year notice, and transitional guarantees for enrolled pupils [evidence: strong — Nkt. (public education act) analysis].
- Since 2013, most state schools are run by state school district centres (tankerület), but churches and foundations maintain a growing share of schools, complicating any uniform structural mandate [evidence: strong — Public education act amendments; maintainer statistics].
- Any modification of church-run school structures must comply with bilateral agreements between the state and recognized churches, restricting unilateral state structural interventions [evidence: moderate — Model knowledge].

## Interpretation
The legal complexity of phasing out early selection is high due to the fragmentation of school maintainers. Implementing a structural change requires complex legislative drafting to respect the autonomy of church and foundation schools, which are shielded by separate concordats and contracts. A "felmenő rendszer" (gradual phase-out) is legally mandatory to avoid violating the constitutional rights of already enrolled students.

## Assumptions
- Maintainers (especially churches) would legally challenge unilateral reduction of their school structures [assumption].
- The constitutional protection of acquired rights (szerzett jogok) prevents the immediate termination of active 6- and 8-year programs [assumption].

## Position
Hungary should not abruptly abolish the 6- and 8-year gimnázium system, but rather initiate a phased transition through legislative amendments that establish uniform structural rules across all maintainers while guaranteeing a gradual ("felmenő rendszer") teach-out path for current students.

## Uncertainties
- The precise willingness of major church maintainers to voluntarily renegotiate their structural agreements in exchange for compensatory funding (megbízhatóság: közepes; csökkentené: a Magyar Katolikus Püspöki Konferencia és a Református Egyház képviselőivel folytatott strukturált interjúk).
- The exact timeframe required to pass constitutional muster for altering foundation school charters (megbízhatóság: alacsony; csökkentené: az Alkotmánybíróság korábbi, szerzett jogokkal kapcsolatos határozatainak jogi elemzése).
