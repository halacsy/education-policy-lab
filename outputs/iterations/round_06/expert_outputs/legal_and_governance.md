# Expert analysis: legal_and_governance

## Findings (evidence)
- The 6/8-year gimnazium forms are anchored in the public education act and maintainer agreements; phase-out requires legislative change, multi-year notice, and transitional guarantees for enrolled pupils. [evidence: strong — Nkt. (public education act) analysis]
- Since 2013 most state schools are run by state school district centres (tankerulet); churches and foundations maintain a growing share of schools, complicating any uniform structural mandate. [evidence: strong — Public education act amendments; maintainer statistics]

## Interpretation
Legislative anchoring and maintainer plurality mean any structural change needs statutory amendment, long notice periods, and negotiated church-maintainer compliance — otherwise a state-only reform just shifts selection to non-state schools.

## Assumptions
- Constitutional protection of acquired pupil status is respected (felmenő rendszer). [assumption]

## Position
Design the legal transition first; it determines the feasible speed.

## Uncertainties
