# Critique: cost_checker
## S1.cost_categories
Objection: The cost estimate for "assessment redesign, psychometric validation and piloting" is labeled as "medium" but the description states it requires "multi-year psychometric development," which suggests a higher cost than typically associated with a medium rating.
Severity: medium
Suggested revision: Re-evaluate the cost category for "assessment redesign, psychometric validation and piloting" to accurately reflect the significant time and resources implied by multi-year development. Consider if "high" is more appropriate, or provide a more granular breakdown of costs if possible.

## S2.cost_categories
Objection: The recurring cost for "parallel-regime administration while grandfathered cohorts run under old rules alongside capped intake" is listed as "medium, transitional." However, the scenario spans a decade, implying this transitional cost will be incurred for a significant period, potentially making it more substantial than a typical "medium" recurring cost.
Severity: medium
Suggested revision: Clarify the expected duration and scale of the "parallel-regime administration" cost. If it is a significant ongoing expense over the 10-year transition, consider if a "high" rating or a more detailed cost breakdown is warranted.

## S3.cost_categories
Objection: The cost for "nationwide teacher retraining in differentiated instruction" is listed as "high" with the justification "no per-cohort/per-district unit cost available." While the lack of a precise unit cost is a valid reason for uncertainty, the "high" classification might be speculative without further context on the scale of retraining required.
Severity: low
Suggested revision: Provide a more detailed explanation or a range for the "high" cost of teacher retraining, even if it's an estimate based on comparable international programs or the number of teachers involved. This would make the cost assessment more concrete.

## S4.cost_categories
Objection: The cost category "TEIP-style targeted funding for disadvantaged schools" is listed as "high" with the justification "no numeric range available; this line is the first cut in fiscal consolidation, so exposure is a serious gap." The justification highlights a significant fiscal risk and lack of quantification, which is critical for a cost assessment.
Severity: high

The current justification indicates a major unknown that significantly impacts the scenario's financial feasibility and should be addressed before proceeding.
