# Critique: cost_checker

## S1.cost_categories
Objection: The cost category 'SES-proxy data infrastructure' lacks a clear fiscal range for the 'high cost' option (administrative data-linkage/register build), making it difficult to assess the full financial implications.
Severity: medium
Suggested revision: Provide a numeric fiscal range (e.g., 'high cost: X-Y million EUR') for the administrative data-linkage/register build option, even if it's an estimate.

## S2.cost_categories
Objection: The cost category 'Compensatory funding or capacity controls for general schools' is described as 'medium-to-high, multi-year, currently unbudgeted' but lacks any numeric fiscal range or duration, making it impossible to evaluate the financial commitment.
Severity: high
Suggested revision: Provide an estimated numeric fiscal range and a more specific duration (e.g., 'X-Y million EUR annually for Z years') for compensatory funding, acknowledging it's unbudgeted.

## S3.cost_categories
Objection: The cost category 'Early diagnostic and remedial support system build-out' is labeled 'high, recurring' but completely lacks any numeric fiscal range or duration, which is crucial for a 'high' cost item.
Severity: high
Suggested revision: Provide an estimated numeric fiscal range (e.g., 'X-Y million EUR annually') and a duration for the recurring costs of the early diagnostic and remedial support system.

## S4.cost_categories
Objection: The cost category 'TEIP-style targeted funding line for disadvantaged schools' explicitly states 'no numeric fiscal range available' and identifies it as a 'likely first target for cuts,' which makes its effective cost and viability highly uncertain.
Severity: high
Suggested revision: Even if uncertain, provide a speculative numeric fiscal range (e.g., 'estimated X-Y million EUR annually') for the TEIP-style funding, and explicitly state the implications of its vulnerability to cuts on the overall cost assessment.
