## Notes for 6/8-year gimnázium research
The statutory basis of 6/8-grade gimnázium (Nkt. 11. §) has survived all
amendment waves; curtailment attempts (e.g. 2014–2016 policy debates on
restricting early-selection gimnázium tracks) never reached enacted amendment
of 11. §. See the domestic-press source directory for the public debate trail.
