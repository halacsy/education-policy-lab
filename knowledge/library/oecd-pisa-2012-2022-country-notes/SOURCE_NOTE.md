# SOURCE_NOTE

**What this is:** OECD PISA country notes for Hungary — official OECD summaries of Hungary's PISA results (performance levels, trends, and equity indicators, including the strong relationship between socio-economic status and performance in Hungary's early-selecting school system).

**Files stored:**
- `pisa-2022-country-note-hungary.pdf` (612 KB, 10 pp.) — "PISA 2022 Results (Volume I and II) — Country Notes: Hungary", © OECD 2023.
  - Exact download URL: https://www.oecd.org/content/dam/oecd/en/publications/reports/2023/11/pisa-2022-results-volume-i-and-ii-country-notes_2fca04b9/hungary_5f1ae58d/3df2ae68-en.pdf
  - Landing page: https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/pisa-2022-results-volume-i-and-ii-country-notes_ed6fbcc5-en/hungary_3df2ae68-en.html
- `pisa-2018-country-note-hungary.pdf` (1.3 MB) — "Hungary — Country Note — PISA 2018 Results (Volumes I–III)", © OECD 2019.
  - Original OECD URL (now offline after the 2024 oecd.org redesign): https://www.oecd.org/pisa/publications/PISA2018_CN_HUN.pdf
  - Exact download URL used (Internet Archive capture of the OECD original): http://web.archive.org/web/20230812050522/https://www.oecd.org/pisa/publications/PISA2018_CN_HUN.pdf

**Not stored — 2012 and 2015 notes:** the PISA 2012 country note ("PISA-2012-results-hungary.pdf") and PISA 2015 country note ("PISA-2015-Hungary.pdf") were hosted at legacy oecd.org URLs that no longer resolve after the OECD website migration and have no Internet Archive captures; they were not easily retrievable. Hungary trend data for those cycles are available via OECD Education GPS (https://gpseducation.oecd.org/CountryProfile?primaryCountry=HUN) and in the PISA 2012/2015 Results volumes on oecd.org.
