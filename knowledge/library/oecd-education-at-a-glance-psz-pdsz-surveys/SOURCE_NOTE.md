# SOURCE_NOTE — OECD Education at a Glance (Hungary) + PSZ/PDSZ surveys

## What
1. `oecd-eag-2025-hungary-country-note.pdf` (362 KB, 9 pages, verified %PDF) —
   OECD "Education at a Glance 2025: Hungary" country note (the latest edition,
   published September 2025; thematic focus on tertiary education, also covers
   spending per student, attainment, teachers). Notable figures: Hungary spends
   USD 6,905/student (primary–post-secondary non-tertiary), low end of OECD;
   65 pp intergenerational tertiary-attainment gap, among the largest in OECD.
2. `POINTER.md` — PSZ and PDSZ teacher-shortage surveys (union/press material,
   pointer-only): PSZ 2023 calculation (20,587 missing teachers, 2020/21),
   PSZ 2025 follow-up (~16,000), PDSZ 2022 and 2025 member surveys.
