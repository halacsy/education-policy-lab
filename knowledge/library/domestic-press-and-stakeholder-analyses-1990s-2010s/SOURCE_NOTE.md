# SOURCE_NOTE — Domestic press and stakeholder analyses (1990s–2010s, with current-debate anchors)

## What
Pointer-only aggregate (`POINTER.md`) of 10 representative, publicly linkable
articles, analyses and stakeholder statements on (a) parental attachment to
the 6/8-year gimnázium forms, (b) past curtailment attempts (1990s
szerkezetváltás regulation, the 2014–2015 nine-grade általános iskola plan),
and (c) the 2026 review debate. One-line summaries only; no article text
reproduced beyond short paraphrase.

## License basis / why pointer-only
Commercial press articles (Index, HVG, Telex, Eduline, Népszava) and
union/association statements are copyright-protected and not redistributable
in a public repository; hvg360 items are additionally paywalled. The OFI and
TÁRKI items are institutionally published and freely accessible at the given
URLs but were kept as pointers for consistency (no redistribution licence
stated). Links + short summaries fall within quotation/citation norms
(Szjt. 34. §).
