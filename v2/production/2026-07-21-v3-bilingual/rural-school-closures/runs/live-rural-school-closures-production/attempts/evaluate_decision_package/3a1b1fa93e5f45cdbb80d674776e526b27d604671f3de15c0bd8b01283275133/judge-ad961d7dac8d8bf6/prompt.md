It is administratively low-friction but has a documented viability floor: a 100-student threshold would affect over 500 village schools, many unable to survive even in split form [F-live-implementation_planning-04], and risks reproducing the between-settlement segregation historically tied to Hungary's parallel-class-reduction practice [F-live-equity_and_social_mobility-03].
